Features

Built for reliability, transparency, and integration into your workflow.

Image & PDF input

Support for JPEG, PNG, WebP, and PDF. Handwritten or printed; single page or multi-page. Optional folder structure (e.g. Topic/Grade/Month) for organizing uploads and jobs.

Structured extraction

Questions are stored as JSON with LaTeX, optional choices and choice labels, and metadata. Same structure whether you upload from the Solver page or the Dashboard.

Verified solver

Every solution is computed via a dedicated math utility‚Äîno "mental math" from the model‚Äîso numeric answers are precise and auditable. Configurable for your workflow.

Step-by-step solutions

Every solution includes numbered steps and a final answer. Steps are parsed and displayed in the UI and in exports (PDF/DOCX). Human-readable fractions and symbols, not raw LaTeX.

Similar questions

Generate similar questions for any extracted item. Useful for practice sets, alternate forms, or item banks. Stored per job and viewable in the app.

Dashboard & jobs

Dashboard for uploads, jobs (questions), and steps. Filter jobs by source file, rename files/folders with automatic relinking of jobs. Delete jobs or files with cascade cleanup.

Export

Download results as PDF (from rendered UI) or as editable DOCX. LaTeX in content is converted to readable text (e.g. 1/3, √ó, ‚àö) in exports.

Configurable models

Use different models for extraction vs. solving (e.g. Opus for vision extraction, Sonnet for solving). Prompts are externalized in YAML for tuning without code changes.
