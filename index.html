What We Do

We support learning—and we maintain knowledge bases.

Thoughtlane builds products that enhance student cognitive ability and reduce teacher burden: personalized practice, open-ended reasoning, assessment, and classroom-ready AI.

Apart from supporting learning, we also maintain knowledge bases. Organizations in any industry can keep large document sets up to date and chat with their entire knowledge base through our product suite, Docket—built for mid to large companies that need authentic answers from their own sources.

Every product is designed as trusted and accurate AI: answers sourced from trusted materials, systems deployed in trusted locations, and cost governance built into the architecture from day one.
