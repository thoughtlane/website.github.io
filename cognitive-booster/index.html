Build understanding, not dependence

Cognitive Booster is the guided-practice mode inside STEM Solver. Students work through problems themselves with hints when they’re stuck and gentle checking as they go—so they learn the reasoning behind each step, not just how to copy a solution.

Open STEM Solver Benefits

Who it’s for

Learners who need support—and adults who want them to keep thinking.

Students

You’re practicing for quizzes or exams and want help that doesn’t skip straight to the answer.

Teachers & tutors

You can’t sit beside every student at once; Cognitive Booster extends your coaching with structured hints and progress you can discuss later.

Parents supporting homework

You’d rather your child build confidence and methods than memorize a single worked example from the back of the book.

Benefits for learners and schools

What makes guided practice different from “show me the solution.”

🪜

Step-by-step thinking

Students record reasoning one step at a time, which mirrors how experts solve problems—and how they’ll need to show work on assessments.

❔

Hints, not spoilers

Support appears as the next nudge, not the full path, so learners still own the “aha” moment.

📊

Visible progress

Students (and teachers) can see how often hints were needed, where steps were corrected, and what was done independently—great for conferences and goal-setting.

🔄

Picks up where you left off

Practice sessions can resume later, supporting busy schedules and multi-day assignments.
