Give every student useful feedback—without burning out

Evaluation & Correction helps teachers and programs review written work and exercises with consistent, clear feedback. You reclaim evenings for planning and coaching; students get corrections they can act on while the assignment is still fresh.

Ask about evaluation Benefits

Who it’s for

Teams where marking volume is high and fairness matters.

Classroom teachers

You have stacks of short answers, problem sets, or digital submissions and need to return work quickly with comments that actually help.

Tutoring & test-prep centers

You promise timely feedback as part of your value; automation-assisted evaluation keeps that promise as you grow.

Departments & PLCs

You want similar rubrics and language across sections so students experience equitable expectations.

Benefits for your school

Why schools adopt evaluation support—stated for decision-makers and teachers.

⚖

More consistent grading

Reduce drift between teachers or sections so the same work earns similar guidance—especially important for high-stakes practice.

⏰

Faster turnaround

Students improve when feedback arrives while they still remember the task—not weeks later.

💡

Clear next steps

Comments point to what to fix or try next, supporting growth mindset and independent revision.

💚

Teacher wellbeing

Less repetitive marking means more energy for the conversations only a human teacher can lead.
