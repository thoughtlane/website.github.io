What is STEM Solver?

STEM Solver is an AI-powered pipeline for education and content teams. Upload photos or PDFs of question sheets (math today; physics, chemistry, and more coming). You get:

  • Extracted questions in structured form (LaTeX, multiple choice, labels)
  • Step-by-step solutions for each question, with a final answer
  • Verified results‚Äîcalculations use a dedicated compute layer so numeric answers are precise and auditable
  • Two modes‚ÄîSolver (see full solutions) or Cognitive Helper (solve yourself with hints and step checking)
  • Similar questions generated on demand for practice or assessment

No need to retype problems; your existing worksheets become the starting point. Export as PDF or editable DOCX.
